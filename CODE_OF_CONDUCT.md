# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in AACC to be respectful, constructive, and welcoming regardless of background, identity, experience level, or the AI tools a contributor uses.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, specific, and open to technical feedback.
- Discuss ideas and evidence rather than attacking people.
- Respect privacy: do not request, publish, or redistribute another person’s credentials, prompts, code, or private task data.
- Help keep security and accessibility concerns visible.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, deliberate exposure of secrets, and disruptive or bad-faith behavior are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to <zhangboqian@hotmail.com>. Reports will be reviewed in good faith; responses may include clarification, a warning, removal of content, or exclusion from project spaces when needed to protect participants.

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and to public behavior when it materially affects the AACC community.
